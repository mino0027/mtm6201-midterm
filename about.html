The Ottawa Bagel is located in downtown Ottawa, and we are dedicated to give customers fresh and local creations centred around the bagel. We are a start up and looking to put Ottawa on the “bagel” map. Ottawa has some of the best fresh bakeries in the world; especially for bagels. Our goal to is to sell fresh produce as well as in house creations to our hungrier customers. With a warm and relaxed vibe, we are family run and 100% local, hoping to put a spotlight on the community that supports us.
